Senior Full-Stack Developer

Stockholm / Remote Full-time

Build the next generation of compliance AI infrastructure. You'll work across the entire stack -- from designing intuitive React interfaces that make complex regulatory data accessible, to architecting Python services that power our AI engine. This is a high-impact role where your code directly shapes how manufacturers worldwide navigate compliance.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and build full-stack features using React, TypeScript, and Python with PostgreSQL
  • Architect scalable APIs and data pipelines that process regulatory information in real time
  • Collaborate with AI/ML engineers to integrate intelligent compliance analysis into the platform
  • Drive technical decisions on architecture, tooling, and engineering best practices
  • Contribute to a culture of code quality through reviews, testing, and documentation

CFO (Chief Financial Officer)

Stockholm Full-time

Drive the financial strategy of a fast-growing AI compliance startup as we scale from early traction to market leadership. You'll own everything from financial planning and SaaS metrics to fundraising and investor relations.   • Partner with the CEO on strategic planning, board presentations, and go-to-market finance

Compliance Expert

Stockholm / Remote Full-time

Shape how AI interprets and applies regulatory requirements across the EU and beyond. You'll bring deep expertise in CE marking, EU directives, and harmonised standards to ensure our platform delivers accurate, trustworthy compliance intelligence. This role bridges the gap between regulatory knowledge and cutting-edge technology.

  • Stay current on regulatory changes and translate them into platform requirements

  • Develop compliance content, guidance documents, and knowledge base resources
