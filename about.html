Our Story

Built on a decade of compliance expertise

From traditional compliance SaaS to cutting-edge AI — a journey shaped by thousands of real-world regulatory workflows.

ProductLex AI was founded as a Nordic company with the mission to digitize and automate product compliance. What started as a traditional compliance SaaS platform over a decade ago has been refined through thousands of real compliance workflows, serving enterprise manufacturers across Europe.

The platform was forged in the trenches of real-world regulatory challenges — CE marking, REACH, RoHS, MDR, and dozens of other frameworks that manufacturers navigate daily. Every feature, every workflow, every decision tree was shaped by actual compliance professionals solving actual problems.

In 2024, with investment backing — including from a venture capital company and Nordic angel investors — the company underwent a transformation. We layered cutting-edge AI, built on a multi-LLM consensus architecture, on top of the battle-tested compliance core. The result is ProductLex AI: a platform that combines deep regulatory domain knowledge with the speed and intelligence of modern AI.
